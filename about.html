About us

Professional care that is reliable at affordable price

Kanoujiya Drycleaners & Laundry Works, established in 2002, is a trusted name in the industry, renowned for its exceptional dry cleaning and laundry services. With nearly two decades of experience, we have perfected our craft, delivering unparalleled care for your garments. Our team of skilled professionals utilizes advanced techniques, state-of-the-art equipment, and eco-friendly solvents to ensure the utmost quality and customer satisfaction. Whether it's delicate fabrics, tailored suits, or everyday clothing, we handle each item with meticulous attention to detail.

Our commitment to excellence, prompt service, and affordable pricing has made us a preferred choice for individuals and businesses alike. Trust Kanoujiya Drycleaners & Laundry Works to make your clothes look and feel their best.
